ABOUT US

ThunderBolt AMS stands for

Acoustics And Media Society. It is

student crew that overlooks the lights,

audio and media related engagements

of all academic and cultural galleries

organised at IIITA.

SERVICES WE PROVIDE

A close knit group of 23 amazing people with a spectacular skill set in the field of photography, editing and much more, the Acoustics and Media Society of IIIT Allahabad, is the backbone of almost all of the instituteâ€™s events.
Our mission is to provide a supportive environment for interested photography students to share their creativity, knowledge and passion for the same.

Photography

Photography is a way of feeling, of touching, of loving. What you have caught on film is captured forever. It remembers little things, long after you have forgotten everything. Thunderbolt tries to capture all of these beautiful crumbs and keep the memories alive.
A portrait is not made in the camera but on either side of it.

VIDEOGRAPHY

Photos seem good enough a souvenir, but videos take the whole thing to a new level. For most purposes photos might be a good enough tool to remember all the sad and happy moments in our life.
Although videos will always take it to a whole new level and keep reminding us how it played out exactly.

Editing

Raw photos will always be the essence of photography!! But just as a person looks better with a little effort, a good photo gets better with just the right filters. We put in that little extra with a plethora of photo-editing softwares and the oh so amazing skills of our amazing team.

Audi Console Handling

Ever felt Awestruck when you were sitting in an auditorium? All the lights which brightens the stage and all the sounds which strike your heart with their beats. That is the power held within its console, And we are the ones who hold it in our hands.
